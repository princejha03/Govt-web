About the Advanced Monitoring System

The Advanced Monitoring System (AMS) is a government-backed initiative aimed at enhancing transportation safety through real-time surveillance, driver behavior analysis, and geographic data visualization.

Smart Surveillance

AI-powered cameras monitor vehicles and detect anomalies for swift response.

Driver Monitoring

Detects drowsiness, distraction, and potential alcohol influence in real time.

Real-Time Geo Mapping

Live vehicle tracking using integrated GPS mapping.
